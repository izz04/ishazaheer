BDAZZLE

Startup Concept Sept-Oct 2019

BDazzle is a mobile-based application that aids you to customize products according to your tastes. The objective of BDazzle is to “Customize E-commerce,” by using the power of business and technology to strengthen communities and empower people around the world to make their own choices. BDazzle serves as a platform for connecting creative entrepreneurs with thoughtful consumers looking for items that are intended to be special.
AWARDS : 'BDazzle' was declared the runner-up of 'Comfest 2019 - Metier Projet', a national level business plan presentation competition for students.
